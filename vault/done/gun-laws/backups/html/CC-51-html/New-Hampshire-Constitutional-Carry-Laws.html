Federal Firearms Regulation

Federal firearm regulation is more limited compared to state authority. New Hampshire‚Äôs Gun¬†Laws

New Hampshire adopts a permissive stance on firearms:

  • No waiting periods for firearm purchases
  • No background checks for private sales
  • No restrictions on assault weapons or ghost guns
  • No prohibition on firearm possession based solely on mental health history, except under active protective orders

Open and Concealed Carry in New Hampshire

In New Hampshire:

  • Open carry of firearms is allowed without a permit
  • Concealed carry does not require a license unless for reciprocity with other states
  • Loaded handguns can be carried in motor vehicles
  • State law preempts local firearm regulations that exceed state law
