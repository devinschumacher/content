  • Registration and Permits: Kansas does not require firearm registration. A permit is not needed to purchase guns.
  • Age Restrictions: Individuals must be 18 to purchase firearms. Minors under 18 may possess firearms under specific circumstances, such as during supervised activities like hunting or competition shooting.
