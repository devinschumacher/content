Johnson and produced by Michael J. Powell. This heartwarming track became a massive hit, charting high on the Billboard Hot 100, Adult Contemporary, and R&B charts in 1986, and ultimately clinched Anita Baker her first Grammy for "Best R&B Song" in 1987.

With its infectious melody and emotive lyrics, "Sweet Love" remains an iconic track in the history of R&B music.
