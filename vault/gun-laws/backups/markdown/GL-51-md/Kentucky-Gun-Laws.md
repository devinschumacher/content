### Firearm Laws in Kentucky

Kentucky firearm laws emphasize Second Amendment rights while enforcing specific regulations:

  * **Registration and Permits:** Firearms do not need to be registered in Kentucky. No permit is required to purchase firearms.
  * **Age Requirements:** Individuals must be at least 18 years old to purchase firearms.
  * **Purchase Eligibility:** Kentucky residents can purchase firearms from licensed dealers or private parties, following state and federal laws.
  * **Possession and Carrying:** Shotguns and rifles can be possessed without a license. A concealed carry license is required for carrying concealed handguns. Open carry of handguns is generally permitted, with exceptions in specific locations.
  * **Concealed Weapons License Eligibility:** Applicants must be at least 21 years old, a Kentucky resident for at least six months (or active military stationed in Kentucky), and meet eligibility criteria including criminal background checks and firearms safety training.



Kentucky‚Äôs laws aim to balance individual rights with public safety, ensuring responsible firearm use and ownership across the state.
