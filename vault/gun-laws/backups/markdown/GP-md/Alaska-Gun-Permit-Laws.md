### Additional Information on Alaska Gun Laws

While Alaska gun laws do not require registration or permits for rifles, shotguns, or handguns, they adhere to federal guidelines and maintain unique state provisions:

  * **Minimum age for firearm purchase is 18, with restrictions on sales to felony convicts and individuals under the influence.**
  * **Possession on school grounds and other specified public places is prohibited without permission.**
  * **Concealed carry of handguns is allowed without a permit for those over 21, with optional permits available for reciprocity in other states.**



These regulations ensure firearms are handled responsibly while upholding Alaskans‚Äô rights under state and federal law.
