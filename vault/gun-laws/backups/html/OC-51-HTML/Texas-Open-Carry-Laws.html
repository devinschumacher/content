No State License for Possession: No state license is required for the possession of rifles, handguns, or shotguns. Restrictions include prohibitions for individuals convicted of felonies or Class A misdemeanors, generally involving crimes against family or household.

Concealed Carry:

Permit Requirements: A concealed handgun license requires an application to the Department of Public Safety, which includes providing completed materials, two passport color photographs, proof of age (at least 21), residency in Texas, completion of a handgun proficiency test by a qualified instructor, an affidavit of understanding, and authorization for records access.

Applicant Disqualifications: Disqualifications include felony or Class A misdemeanor records, addiction to drugs or alcohol, mental illness history, or delinquent tax or child support payments.
